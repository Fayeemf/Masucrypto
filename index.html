Cryptocurrency is the future of our money. What cryptocurrency is, is it’s basically a digital form of money. Not to mention a secure form of money. In a short sense crypto is a digital form of currency. They’re profitable to invest in depending on which one. They’re the new step into the world, where we are migrating from paper to digital.
Like every newly developed innovation there are pros and cons. Lots of advocates of Cryptocurrency will tell you that it is the best financial system ever invented and that it has no faults. On the other hand, some people will point to examples such as the illegal deep-web market Silk Road to try and convince you that it is just a tool for crime. One of the cons of crypto is that it’s easier for people to skirt the law. Many third party organizations are involved with cryptocurrency. However, this can also be comprehended as pro because of secure crypto currency is.
